SpaceX Website

Interactive SpaceX Website which shows the images and decription of projects of SpaceX company

Project Overview

An interactive SpaceX website that showcases detailed descriptions and images of SpaceX's projects, missions, and technological advancements.

Key Features

  • Mission Updates: Real-time updates on ongoing and upcoming SpaceX missions, including launch dates and locations.
  • Image Gallery: High-quality images and videos of SpaceX spacecraft, launches, and engineering innovations.
  • News & Announcements: Latest news, press releases, and event announcements related to SpaceX's activities.
  • Educational Resources: Informational sections explaining SpaceX's technologies and goals, making it educational for visitors.
